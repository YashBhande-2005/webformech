Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your customer support hours?

Our customer support is available 24/7 for emergencies. For general inquiries, our team is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 9 PM, and Saturday 9 AM to 6 PM.

How quickly do you respond to support requests?

Emergency requests are handled immediately. For general support, we respond within 4 hours during business hours and within 24 hours on weekends.

Can I track my service request?

Yes! You can track your service request in real-time through our mobile app or by calling our support number with your booking ID.

How can I become a partner mechanic?

We're always looking for qualified mechanics to join our network. Contact us through the partnership inquiry form or email partners@urbanmechanic.com

Do you offer corporate services?

Yes, we offer specialized packages for corporate fleet management. Contact our business development team for more information.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with any service, contact us immediately and we'll make it right or provide a full refund.
