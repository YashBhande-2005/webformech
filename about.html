Our Story

Urban Mechanic was born from a simple yet powerful idea: vehicle breakdowns shouldn't leave you stranded and helpless in Mumbai's busy streets. Founded in 2025 by a team of automotive enthusiasts and tech innovators, we recognized the unique challenges of vehicle service in Mumbai's dense traffic and diverse neighborhoods.

Our founders experienced firsthand the frustration of waiting hours for help during vehicle emergencies in Mumbai, dealing with unreliable service providers who didn't understand the city's geography, and the anxiety of not knowing when help would arrive through Mumbai's complex traffic patterns. This led to the creation of Urban Mechanic â€“ a platform specifically designed for Mumbai that connects vehicle owners with local, certified mechanics at the tap of a button.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of customers across all Mumbai areas, from South Mumbai to Thane, providing fast, reliable, and transparent vehicle services that understand the unique needs of Mumbai's vehicle owners.

Our Mission

To make vehicle maintenance and emergency services accessible, transparent, and reliable for every vehicle owner in Mumbai. We aim to eliminate the stress and uncertainty associated with vehicle breakdowns in Mumbai's challenging traffic conditions by providing instant access to qualified local professionals.

Our Vision

To become Mumbai's most trusted platform for vehicle services, creating a network where help is always just minutes away across all areas from Colaba to Borivali. We envision a future where no Mumbai resident has to worry about being stranded due to vehicle problems, whether they're stuck in Bandra traffic or broken down on the Eastern Express Highway.
