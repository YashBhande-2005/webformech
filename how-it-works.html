Simple 3-Step Process

From problem to solution in minutes

1

Diagnose Your Issue

Tell us what's wrong with your vehicle or use our AI-powered diagnostic tool to identify the problem. Whether it's a dead battery, flat tire, or engine trouble, we'll help you understand what needs fixing.

  • AI-powered problem detection
  • Photo-based diagnostics
  • Expert consultation available
2

Book a Certified Mechanic

Browse available mechanics in your Mumbai area, check their ratings and reviews, and book the one that best fits your needs. All our mechanics are certified, local to Mumbai, and background-verified for your safety.

  • Mumbai-local certified professionals
  • Real ratings and reviews from Mumbai customers
  • Transparent pricing with no hidden charges
3

Track Arrival & Pay

Watch your mechanic's real-time location as they navigate Mumbai traffic to reach you. Our system accounts for Mumbai's traffic patterns to give accurate ETAs. Once the job is complete, pay securely through the app.

  • Mumbai traffic-aware GPS tracking
  • Secure payment options including UPI
  • Digital receipts and service history
